Dull

Definitions
not sharp; blunt: A dull knife.
causing boredom; tedious; uninteresting: a dull sermon.
not lively or spirited; listless.: a dull attitude.
not bright, intense, or clear; dim: a dull day; a dull sound.
having very little depth of color; lacking in richness or intensity of color. a dull shade of brown.
slow in motion or action; not brisk; sluggish: a dull day in the stock market.
mentally slow; lacking brightness of mind; somewhat stupid; obtuse. a dull person.
lacking keenness of perception in the senses or feelings; insensible; unfeeling. a dull feeling
not intense or acute: a dull pain.
to make or become dull. the knife became dull.
